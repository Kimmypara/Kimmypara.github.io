Ginger Chocolate Cupcakes

45 minutes
Serves 12
Easy

These Ginger Chocolate Cupcakes are delicious, Christmassy and amazingly light. They are ideal for Christmas meal dessert.

INGREDIENTS

240ml vegetable oil
1 cup golden syrup
1½ cup packed brown sugar
4 large eggs
½ cup buttermilk
2 cups self-raising flour
1 cup cocoa powder
1½ tsp baking soda
1tsp salt
6tsp fresh grated ginger
2tsp cinnamon
½ tsp ground cloves
½ tsp nutmeg

FOR CREAM FROSTING

250ml double cream light(must be cold)
4tbsp icing sugar
1tsp vanilla
4tbsp chocolate chips
1tsp cinnamon powder

METHOD

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 180°C
  2. In a saucepan over low heat, melt the oil, golden syrup, brown sugar with ¼ cup water until the sugar dissolves.
  3. Let cool for 5 minutes.
  4. Pour the mixture in an electric mixer(be sure it is not hot to avoid scrambling the eggs) and add the buttermilk and eggs. Whisk until all ingredients are combined.
  5. Add the dry ingredients slowly to wet mixture. DO NOT OVERBEAT.
  6. Place cupcake liners in a cupcake mould tray and pour the batter evenly.
  7. Bake for around 20 minutes or until skewer is clean.
  8. For the cream frosting, pour the cold double cream in a bowl with the vanilla extract.
  9. Whisk for around 1 minute and add the icing sugar slowly.
  10. Keep beating until the cream is thick enough for piping DO NOT OVERBEAT.
  11. Once the cupcakes are cold, put the frosting in a piping bag and decorate.
  12. Sprinkle some cinnamon powder and chocolate chips.
